Welcome to New Bharat Printing Press!!


Established in 1990, New Bharat Printing Press is one of the pioneers and widely acknowledged printing company in Nawada Bihar, with a proven track record in producing high quality print works with originality within a wide range of print materials from Office Stationeries, Point of Sale Materials ( POS ) like Posters, Danglers, Promotional items and high end packaging materials with proficiency and expertise in hand made designs ( please refer photo gallery for different product samples ) and other allied materials specializing in high quality printing. We are also known for our capabilities to produce packaging materials of food grade packaging as well as for toiletries, perfumes and corrugated boxes of various sizes for dispatch and stocking
